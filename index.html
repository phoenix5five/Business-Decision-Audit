Section 1: Current Decision-Making Environment

1. What types of decisions do you make most frequently?

E.g., strategic, operational, financial – provide context.

2. What’s the biggest decision you’re facing right now?

Describe in 3-4 sentences, including stakes and context.

3. How effective is your current decision-making process (1-10)?

Explain your rating with a brief rationale.

4. What resources support your decisions?

E.g., team input, data, software – list key elements.

Section 2: Past Performance Analysis

5. What’s one decision that led to a significant success?

Detail the decision and what made it effective.

6. What’s one costly decision you regret?

Provide specifics – what failed and what was the cost?

7. How do you measure decision outcomes?

E.g., ROI, time saved, team morale – describe your approach.

8. What data or insights do you wish you had?

Pinpoint gaps in your current information flow.

Section 3: Decision-Making Bottlenecks

9. What most often delays your decisions?

E.g., lack of consensus, insufficient data, fear of failure.

10. What risks most inhibit your decision-making?

E.g., financial loss, reputation – rank top 1-2.

11. Which decisions are you currently postponing?

List 1-2 and explain why they’re on hold.

Section 4: Goals and Strategic Alignment

12. What are your top 3 business goals for the next 12 months?

Be specific – e.g., revenue targets, expansion plans.

13. How do your current decisions align with these goals?

Identify synergies or conflicts.

14. What key decisions must you make to achieve these goals?

List 1-2 critical choices ahead.

Section 5: Business Context and Scale

15. What’s the scale of your business?

Revenue range, employee count, industry.

16. What are your top operational challenges?

E.g., supply chain, staffing, tech – list 1-2.

17. What drives your revenue and costs?

Highlight your main income sources and expense drivers.
